Prauda

#PRO

Here's what they needed

As the 2022 school leavers, the 2022 A/L, and 2019 O/L batch desired to organize a get-together. They required promotional materials including social media posts, tickets, photobooth banners, and a promotional video. The social media posts comprised of a main announcement post informing about the event and stating that tickets would be available soon. They also planned a post to notify the availability of tickets, followed by a series of posts counting down the remaining days (a total of 9 posts).


Considering that their school is named after a king in Sri Lanka (Parakramabahu), and the event is called "Prauda," which signifies majesty, they aimed for an ancient royal aesthetic in the design of the posts.
